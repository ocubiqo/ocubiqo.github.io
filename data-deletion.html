Data Deletion Request

2 Sides App — Last updated: June 2026

If you have used the 2 Sides app and wish to request deletion of any data associated with your use of the app or our Facebook Page, you can do so by contacting us directly.

What data we hold

2 Sides does not require account registration. The app may collect:

  • Anonymous usage analytics (device type, app version, session events) via Google Firebase Analytics
  • Crash reports via Google Firebase Crashlytics
  • Advertising identifiers for ad personalisation via Google AdMob (if consent was granted)
  • Push notification tokens (to deliver WC2026 match reminders you opted into)

We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information through the app itself.

How to request deletion

Send an email to ocubiqo@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request – 2 Sides".

Please include your device type (iOS / Android) and approximate date of last use so we can locate any associated records. We will process your request within 30 days and confirm by email once complete.

Deleting data directly on your device

  • Push notifications: Disable in your device's notification settings for the 2 Sides app, or uninstall the app — this removes the push token from our system automatically.
  • Ad personalisation: Reset your Advertising ID in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking; Android: Settings → Google → Ads).
  • Analytics & crash data: Uninstalling the app stops any further collection. To delete historical records contact us at the email above.

Facebook / Meta data

If you interacted with the 2 Sides Facebook Page (likes, comments, messages), that data is held by Meta Platforms, Inc. You can manage or delete it through your Facebook account settings or by using Meta's Data Deletion tool.
