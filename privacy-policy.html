Privacy Policy

App name: 2 Sides  |  Company: Ocubiqo  |  Effective date: April 26, 2026  |  Last updated: May 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Ocubiqo ("we", "our", or "us") operates the 2 Sides mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By downloading or using 2 Sides, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

2 Sides does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use the App.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

Device and Usage Data
When you use the App, we may collect limited technical information including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • General usage patterns (e.g. which features are used)

This data is collected in aggregate and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Local Storage Only
All user preferences and app state are stored exclusively on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage). This includes:

  • Selected conflict / region preference
  • Color theme preference
  • Notification preferences
  • Simulation history
  • News article cache
  • Simulation run count

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

2.3 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Name, email address, or contact information
  • Location data (precise or coarse)
  • Camera or microphone data
  • Phone state or call logs
  • Contacts or calendar data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Biometric data

3. How We Use Information

Any technical data we receive is used solely to:

  • Maintain and improve App performance and stability
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Understand aggregate feature usage to guide development

We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

4.1 Anthropic Claude API

The App uses the Claude API (provided by Anthropic, PBC) to:

  • Fetch and summarise real-time news articles about active conflicts
  • Generate analytical narratives following simulation runs

When these features are active, a query containing country names and simulation parameters is sent to the Anthropic API. No personal user data is included in these requests. Anthropic's privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

4.2 GitHub (Content Delivery)

Aggregated news content and military data are fetched from a publicly accessible GitHub repository operated by Ocubiqo. No user data is sent to GitHub as part of this process.

4.3 Google AdMob

The App includes rewarded video advertisements served by Google AdMob (Google LLC). AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising ID (resettable device identifier)
  • Approximate location (country/region level, derived from IP address)
  • App interaction data for ad targeting and measurement

You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising → Personalised Ads (off)

Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

4.4 Google Firebase Analytics

The App uses Firebase Analytics (Google LLC) to collect aggregate, anonymised usage data including:

  • Screen views and navigation patterns
  • Feature interaction events (e.g. which simulations are run)
  • Session duration and engagement metrics

This data is collected in aggregate and is not linked to any personally identifiable information. Firebase's privacy policy is available at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

4.5 Expo / React Native Infrastructure

The App is built on the Expo platform (Expo, Inc.). Expo may collect limited diagnostic data as part of standard platform operation. Expo's privacy policy is available at expo.dev/privacy.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable war-news reminders, the App will schedule local push notifications on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve transmitting your data to any server. You can disable notifications at any time via:

  • In-App: Settings screen → News Reminders toggle
  • Device: Settings → Notifications → 2 Sides

6. Children's Privacy

2 Sides is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect any data associated with the App. Since user preferences and history are stored only on your device, your data security is largely governed by your device's own security features (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

Data transmitted to third-party APIs (Section 4) is sent over encrypted HTTPS connections.

8. Data Retention

All data stored locally on your device persists until you:

  • Clear the App's cache or storage via device settings
  • Uninstall the App

We do not maintain any server-side user profiles or databases linked to individual users.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access – know what data is held about you
  • Deletion – request deletion of your data
  • Portability – receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out – opt out of personalised advertising (see Section 4.3)

Because 2 Sides stores all data locally on your device and does not maintain user accounts, you can exercise most of these rights directly by clearing App data or uninstalling the App. For any other requests, contact us at the address below.

10. International Users

2 Sides is operated from and governed by the laws applicable to Ocubiqo's place of business. If you access the App from outside that jurisdiction, your information (to the extent any is processed) may be transferred to and processed in a different country. By using the App, you consent to such transfer.

11. Educational and Hypothetical Content

All conflict simulations and military analyses provided by 2 Sides are strictly hypothetical and educational in nature. The App does not advocate any real-world military action. Content accuracy depends on publicly available sources and probabilistic models; it should not be relied upon for any real-world decision-making.
